About This Tool

Deal Scanner — Pulls live adjudicated property data directly from St. Landry Parish records. Every property here is already in a motivated seller situation by definition.


Parcel Lookup — Look up any parcel in St. Landry Parish by number. Find a parcel on the parish map, note the number, and paste it here for a full investment analysis.


Scoring — Deals are scored 0–100 based on motivated seller signals: adjudication status, heirs/estate ownership, out-of-state owners, acreage, and other flags pulled directly from parish data.


Data source: St. Landry Parish ArcGIS FeatureServer. Updated by the parish.
